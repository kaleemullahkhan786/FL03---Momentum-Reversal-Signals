# FL03 - Momentum & Reversal Signals

## Overview
**FL03** is a specialized trend momentum and reversal indicator that cuts through the noise. By employing a unique Average True Range (ATR)-based algorithmic filter, it precisely plots highly accurate Buy and Sell entry arrows at the tipping points of market movements. It acts as an incredibly potent signal generator or confirmation dashboard for any trading strategy.

## Key Features
- **No-Nonsense Entry Signals:** Delivers explicit directional arrows to pinpoint trading opportunities with absolute clarity.
- **Volatility Adjusted:** Evaluates recent market extremes dynamically using ATR to filter out false signals during ranging or choppy environments.
- **Immediate Push Alerts:** Configurable popup alerts ensure you never miss a newly formed signal while watching your charts.
- **Lightweight & Fast:** Engineered with an optimized codebase to ensure completely lag-free performance across all your trading pairs.

## How to Install
1. Download the compiled files (`.ex4` for MT4 or `.ex5` for MT5) from this repository.
2. Open your MetaTrader platform.
3. Click on `File` -> `Open Data Folder` in the top menu.
4. For MT4, navigate to the `MQL4/Indicators` folder. For MT5, navigate to the `MQL5/Indicators` folder.
5. Paste the downloaded files into the folder.
6. Refresh the "Navigator" panel in MetaTrader, or simply restart the platform.
7. Find the indicator in the Navigator, drag and drop it onto your desired chart, and start trading!
